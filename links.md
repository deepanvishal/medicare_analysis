2. Pediatric provider exclusion
CMS MMP HSD Network Submission Instructions CY2024:
https://www.cms.gov/files/document/mmphsdnetworksubmissioninstructionscy2024.pdf
Verbatim: "physicians and specialists must not be pediatric providers; as they do not routinely provide services to the Medicare-population." Quest Analytics
Same language in the CY2019 version:
https://www.cms.gov/Medicare-Medicaid-Coordination/Medicare-and-Medicaid-Coordination/Medicare-Medicaid-Coordination-Office/FinancialAlignmentInitiative/MMPInformationandGuidance/Downloads/HSDNetworkSubmissionInstructionsCY2019.pdf
February 2018 CMS Network Adequacy Guidance also states:
"long-term care, and pediatric/children's hospitals, as well as providers/facilities contracted with the organization only for its commercial, Medicaid, or other products, do not count toward" network adequacy. Attacconsulting
https://www.cms.gov/files/document/medicare-advantage-and-section-1876-cost-plan-network-adequacy-guidancepdf

Both exclusion rules are in the same 2018 guidance document. The pediatric provider exclusion applies to both individual providers and facilities.
